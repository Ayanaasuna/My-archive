Animes

A collection of animes dear to me

Evangelion

Genre: Psychological, Mecha, Drama, Sci-Fi

Explores a world under threat where teenagers pilot giant bio-mechs, focusing heavily on identity, trauma, and philosophy.

Junji Ito

Genre: Horror, Psychological, Supernatural

Anthology of unsettling short stories that dive into weird, nightmarish, and surreal body-horror themes.

Serial Experiment Lain

Genre: Psychological, Cyberpunk, Mystery

A slow-burn exploration of technology, identity, and reality as a girl becomes entangled with a mysterious digital network.

Vampire Knight

Genre: Supernatural, Romance, Drama

Set in a school where humans and vampires coexist, focusing on love triangles and hidden histories.

Dear Brother

Genre: Drama, Psychological, School

A girl enters an elite all-girls school and navigates intense friendships, secrets, and emotional turmoil.

Inuyasha

Genre: Adventure, Romance, Supernatural, Fantasy

A modern-day girl is pulled into a historical fantasy world full of demons, quests, and a strong emotional journey.

Vampire in the Garden

Genre: Fantasy, Drama, Post-apocalyptic

A human girl and a vampire queen embark on a journey to find a place where both species can coexist peacefully.

Spirited Away

Genre: Fantasy, Adventure

A magical coming-of-age story about a girl who enters a spirit world filled with gods, creatures, and mysterious magic.

The Apothecary Diaries

Genre: Historical, Mystery, Drama

A sharp-witted girl works in the imperial palace investigating medical mysteries and uncovering court intrigue.

Dragoball

Genre: Action, Adventure, Martial Arts, Comedy

Follows a young martial artist searching for magical artifacts while training, fighting rivals, and going on wild adventures.
