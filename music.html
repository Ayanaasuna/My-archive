Founders of heavy metal; dark, blues-influenced riffs and iconic vocals.

Kreator

Genre: Thrash metal

German thrash legends known for high-speed riffs and raw political intensity.

Pantera

Power-groove riffs, aggressive attitude, and defining 90s metal sound.

One of the “Big Four”; fast thrash roots evolving into mainstream heavy metal.
